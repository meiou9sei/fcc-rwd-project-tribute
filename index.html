Who was カメババ?

カメババ (read: kamebaba, "Turtle Grandmother") was a lovely woman who passed away at the age of 142 on July 9, 2021*. She was a loving Grandmother, former President, and constructed the first Denny's on the Moon. She also reestablished Pluto as the 9th planet of our solar system, created world hunger so she could end it, established the concept of fast food restaurants, and is generally regarded as the pinnacle of the Panzermensch species, better than even that bastard Canadian Panzermensch Franklin.
